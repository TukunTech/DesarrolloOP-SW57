About us

TukunTech, we are committed to the health and safety of the most vulnerable patients. Our advanced monitoring system gives you peace of mind by ensuring continuous and accurate tracking of your loved ones' vital signs, both at home and in the hospital. With cutting-edge technology, we provide real-time alerts so that medical personnel can act quickly in case of emergency. Our comprehensive approach not only optimizes healthcare but also reduces the manual workload of medical staff, allowing for more efficient management and a higher quality of care. With TukunTech, you can trust that every patient is getting the care they deserve, 24/7.

Frequently questions

Our platform uses a network of smart devices that continuously measure vital signs such as heart rate, blood pressure and blood oxygenation. These devices are connected through IoT technology and transmit data in real time to a centralized platform. There, the data is processed and analyzed using advanced algorithms to detect anomalies. When critical changes in vital signs are identified, the system sends immediate alerts to medical staff for rapid intervention. Additionally, the platform allows doctors to make assessments and adjustments based on instantly updated data.

Our solution overcomes the limitations of traditional systems by providing seamless integration between devices and centralized real-time monitoring. Unlike fragmented, static systems that require manual intervention, our system automates data collection and analysis, improving efficiency and responsiveness. Integration with IoT technology allows for continuous monitoring and allows anomalies to be detected early, facilitating more proactive and personalized medical care. Additionally, the centralized platform improves decision-making accuracy and optimizes patient care management.

Our solution is designed especially for critical environments such as intensive care units, post-operative clinics and for monitoring older adults seeking more advanced care. These environments benefit greatly from an integrated, real-time solution due to the need for constant monitoring and rapid responses to changes in vital signs. Clinics that manage chronic conditions or require precise daily monitoring will find our platform particularly useful in improving quality of care and efficiency in patient management.

Our platform is designed to seamlessly integrate with clinics' existing patient management and healthcare systems. We use standard interfaces and communication protocols to ensure efficient compatibility with current technological infrastructures. During the implementation process, we work closely with the clinics' technical staff to tailor the integration to their specific systems, ensuring the transition is as seamless as possible and continuity in patient care management is maintained. This integration allows monitoring data to be easily incorporated into electronic medical records and other systems used by medical staff, improving coordination and quality of care.
